Addiator Summary
Maker: Unknown
Origin: Hong Kong, c.1960-1970
Description

The 'W Accurator' addiator calculator is a single side device made from a silver coated aluminium.

The device is used for both addition and subtraction and can be divided into two sections, split by six circular accumulator windows in the centre. Each section consists of six rows of vertical teeth, with numerals '0:9' on the right. The upper section is labelled '+ ADDITION', while the lower section has '- SUBTRACTION'. There is a metal reset bar at the top, while below there is a table of 'DECIMAL EQUIVALENTS'.

Other Information
Dimensions (HxWxD mm): 160 x 37 x 3
